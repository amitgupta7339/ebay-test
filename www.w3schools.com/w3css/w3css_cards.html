Hover Effect

The w3-hover-shadow class adds a shadow effect on hover - this will make any element look like a card on mouse-over (same style as w3-card-4).

Hover over me!

Example

<div class="w3-green w3-hover-shadow w3-center">
  <p>Hover over me!</p>
</div>
